# Strategic Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Single Channel Automation
- End-to-end automation
- Minimal human intervention

## Phase 2 — Multi-Channel Scaling
- Shared pipeline
- Channel-specific configs

## Phase 3 — SaaSization
- Template-based pipelines
- External user onboarding

## Phase 4 — Full Agent Orchestration
- Revenue prediction models
- Autonomous optimization loops

## Phase 5 — Research & Benchmarking: YouTube Algorithm Analysis
- Focus on high-CPM educational content in Finance, Economics, History, and Education categories.
- Identify top-performing videos based on view-to-subscriber ratios.
- Identify channels with the fastest subscriber growth.
- Extract and analyze video metadata (title, thumbnail keywords, script, description).
- Utilize Gemini model for virality analysis and winning hook/visual strategy identification.
- Store all benchmarking data for strategic content creation.
